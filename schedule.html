BOXFIT

Skill Level

High intensity conditioning session that is split 50/50.

50% bagwork / 50% conditioning.

Expect lots of body weight and HIIT drills.

BAG WORK

Skill Level

Primary focus on learning to use the bag to increase your skill level.

Footwork, long range, short range, working the head and body.

Mainly focused on the bag but not limited to it, high intensity.

SPARRING

Skill Level

For the more advanced boxers.

Gumshields and Head Guards essential.

Quick warm up and then open spar in the ring!

CONDITIONING

Skill Level

Work through a circuit of stations.

Body weight, free weights and machines.

High and low intensity for cardio and strength.

SKILL SCHOOL

Skill Level

For intermediate boxers and above.

Improve footwork, ring craft, speed, timing and distance.

Technical sparring can be epxected so bring a gum sheild!
